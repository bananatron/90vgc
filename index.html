FAQ

Are there any rules?
  1. Be kind and respect others. Trolling, hate speech, othering, or general asshattery of any kind is prohibited.
How does 90VDC communicate?

We communicate primarily via discord. You'll receive an invite to the channel upon registration.

How does 90VDC decide which games to play?

We vote on the next game to play. The game which meets the voting requirements with the most votes is added to the list.
