Why do you need my email?

We use email to send group communication about upcoming game selection and voting. We'll never sell/trade/lease your email to any other entity.

The 90's Video Game Club is like a book club, but for video games. Each month, we'll casually play through a PC game from the 90s and discuss the work. We welcome those playing through these games for the first time, as well as players looking to refuel theirn nostalgia.
